Formal Methods for Quantum Programs

Tensor Products

Mathematically, to understand multiparticle systems in quantum mechanics, it is necessary to be able to construct a Hilbert space H that is a composite of the independent Hilbert spaces that are associated with each individual particle.

Hilbert space: A Hilbert space H is a real or complex inner product space that is also a complete metric space with respect to the distance function induced by the inner product.
